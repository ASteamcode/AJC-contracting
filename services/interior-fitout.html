Interior Fit-Out in Lebanon

Custom joinery, stone cladding, MEP integration and luxury finishes for premium spaces.

About This Service

Lebanese interior design is known worldwide for its blend of tradition and luxury. AJC's fit-out team combines Lebanese craftsmanship — hand-carved stone, traditional arched windows, bespoke woodwork — with modern MEP systems, smart lighting and high-end imported finishes.

AJC Contracting operates across all Lebanese governorates — Beirut, Metn, Jounieh, Tripoli, Sidon, Bekaa and beyond. Our team of licensed OEA engineers and skilled tradespeople delivers every project on time and on budget.
