Our Story

Built On Lebanese
Pride & Precision

AJC Contracting was founded in 2009 with one clear mission: to deliver construction excellence across Lebanon. From the mountain towns of Metn to the heart of Beirut and the Bekaa Valley, we have built our reputation one project at a time.

Through economic uncertainty, seismic challenges, and the resilience that defines Lebanon, we continued building — not just concrete and steel, but lasting relationships with clients who trust us with their most important investments.

Today, AJC operates as a full-service general contractor with an in-house team of OEA-registered engineers, certified electricians, and experienced project managers who bring international standards to every Lebanese worksite.

How We Work

Our Project Approach

A structured, client-focused process from the first conversation to final handover — designed to eliminate surprises and deliver results.

01

Consultation & Feasibility

We begin with a free site visit and consultation to understand your vision, budget, and timeline. Our engineers assess feasibility and provide honest guidance from day one.

02

Detailed Quotation & Planning

You receive a comprehensive, line-item quotation and a milestone-based project schedule. No vague estimates — full transparency before any work begins.

03

Execution & Site Management

Our on-site engineers and project managers oversee every phase of construction, coordinating subcontractors and materials to maintain quality and momentum.

04

Quality Control & Inspection

Structured quality checks at every milestone ensure work meets our standards — and yours. We do not move forward until each stage passes inspection.
